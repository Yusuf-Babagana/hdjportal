Our Programs

Quality healthcare education programs designed to produce competent professionals

Diploma in Community Health (SCHEW)

This program prepares Senior Community Health Extension Workers (SCHEW) who provide primary healthcare services in communities, health centers, and clinics.

Career Opportunities:
  • Primary Healthcare Centers
  • Community Health Programs
  • Public Health Departments
  • NGOs and International Organizations

Duration: 2 Years
Entry Requirement: SSCE with 5 credits including English, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics

Certificate in Community Health (JCHEW)

This program trains Junior Community Health Extension Workers (JCHEW) to assist in providing basic healthcare services at the community level.

Career Opportunities:
  • Community Health Centers
  • Rural Health Posts
  • Maternal and Child Health Programs
  • Immunization Programs

Duration: 18 Months
Entry Requirement: SSCE with 5 credits including English, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics

Diploma in Health Information Management

This program focuses on the management of patient health information and medical records in healthcare facilities using both manual and electronic systems.

Career Opportunities:
  • Hospitals and Clinics
  • Health Insurance Companies
  • Medical Records Departments
  • Health Data Management Organizations

Duration: 2 Years
Entry Requirement: SSCE with 5 credits including English, Mathematics, and 3 other relevant subjects

Diploma in Environmental Health

This program trains environmental health officers to identify, assess, and control environmental factors that may affect public health.

Career Opportunities:
  • Environmental Health Departments
  • Water and Sanitation Programs
  • Food Safety and Inspection Services
  • Occupational Health and Safety

Duration: 2 Years
Entry Requirement: SSCE with 5 credits including English, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics

Diploma in X-Ray and Imaging

This program trains radiographers to operate imaging equipment and assist in diagnostic procedures using various medical imaging technologies.

Career Opportunities:
  • Hospital Radiology Departments
  • Diagnostic Imaging Centers
  • Mobile X-Ray Services
  • Private Medical Clinics

Duration: 2 Years
Entry Requirement: SSCE with 5 credits including English, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics

Diploma in Nutrition and Dietetics

This program trains nutritionists and dietitians to provide nutrition education, meal planning, and dietary counseling to promote health and manage diseases.

Career Opportunities:
  • Hospital Nutrition Departments
  • Community Nutrition Programs
  • School Feeding Programs
  • Private Nutrition Consulting

Duration: 2 Years
Entry Requirement: SSCE with 5 credits including English, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, and one other science subject

Retraining in Community Health (JCHEW Holders)

This program is designed for current JCHEW holders who want to upgrade their skills and knowledge to SCHEW level. It provides advanced training in community health practices.

Who Can Apply:
  • Current JCHEW certificate holders
  • Minimum 2 years work experience as JCHEW
  • Employed in a recognized health facility

Duration: 18 Months
Mode: Part-time/Weekend
Certification: SCHEW Diploma

General Admission Requirements

Academic Requirements
  • Senior Secondary School Certificate (SSCE)
  • Minimum of 5 credits at not more than 2 sittings
  • Must include English Language and Mathematics
  • Science subjects: Biology, Chemistry, Physics (for science programs)
Required Documents
  • SSCE Results (original and photocopies)
  • Primary School Certificate
  • Birth Certificate or Declaration of Age
  • Certificate of Origin/Indigene Certificate
  • Passport photographs
