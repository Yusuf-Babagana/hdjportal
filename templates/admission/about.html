About CHSTH

College of Health Sciences and Technology Hadejia

Our Mission

To provide quality healthcare education and training that produces competent, skilled, and compassionate healthcare professionals who will serve communities across Nigeria and beyond.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in healthcare education, recognized for excellence in training healthcare professionals and contributing to improved health outcomes in our communities.

Core Values

  • Excellence - Commitment to high standards in education and practice
  • Integrity - Honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct
  • Innovation - Embracing new technologies and methodologies
  • Service - Dedication to serving communities and humanity

Our History

Establishment

The College of Health Sciences and Technology Hadejia was established to address the critical need for qualified healthcare professionals in Jigawa State and the broader northern Nigeria region.

Growth & Development

Over the years, CHSTH has grown to become a respected institution, producing thousands of healthcare professionals who serve in various capacities across the healthcare sector.

Our Facilities

State-of-the-art facilities to support quality healthcare education

Modern Laboratories

Well-equipped laboratories for practical training in various health sciences disciplines.

Library & Resources

Comprehensive library with extensive collection of healthcare literature and digital resources.

Clinical Training

Partnership with healthcare facilities for hands-on clinical experience and training.
